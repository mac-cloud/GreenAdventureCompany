Past Events

Kanunga WaterFall

Kanunga Waterfall is a hidden gem located in Kiambu County, Kenya, near the town of Limuru.
Surrounded by lush greenery and farmland, the waterfall offers a serene escape with its cascading waters and tranquil atmosphere.
It's a great spot for nature walks, photography, and quiet picnics,
making it a favorite among local adventurers and visitors seeking off-the-beaten-path experiences.
